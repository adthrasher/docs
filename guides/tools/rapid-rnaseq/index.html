Rapid RNA-Seq Fusion Detection¶

  • Authors: Scott Newman, Clay McLeod, Yongjin Li
  • Publication: N/A (not published)
  • Technical Support: Contact Us

Introduction

Fusion genes are important for cancer diagnosis, subtype definition and targeted therapy. RNASeq is useful for detecting fusion transcripts. Computational methods face challenges to identify fusion transcripts arising from internal tandem duplication (ITD), multiple genes, low expression or non-templated insertions. Here we present an end-to-end clinically validated pipeline “Rapid RNA-Seq” that detects gene fusions and ITDs from human transcriptome data.
